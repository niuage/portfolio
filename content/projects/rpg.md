A top-down RPG prototype with an isometric camera perspective. The game featured a character navigating through grassy environments, interacting with glowing orbs and geometric structures. It was an early experiment in building RPG mechanics — movement, basic combat, and environmental storytelling — all within a moody, muted color palette.
