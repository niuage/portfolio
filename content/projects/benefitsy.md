**Benefitsy** was a web app designed to help people learn about the health benefits of fruits, vegetables, and herbs. Users could explore a catalog of produce with beautiful botanical illustrations, browse recipes, and search for specific ingredients.

Clean, simple, and focused on making nutrition information approachable.
